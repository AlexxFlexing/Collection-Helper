For each map in a collection, a new one will be generated with your selected difficulty overrides. If you choose to override BPM, new audio files will be generated. If a map has multiple timing points, the most common BPM of a map (seen in osu! in brackets) will be used as a base, and every other timing point will be scaled accordingly. A new collection will be created with all of the newly generated maps. Note: If the generated maps do not appear in game, you will have to refresh your maps by pressing F5 in osu!.
